03

Everyone Welcome

Come once, or come every day.

Kids, parents, grandparents and neighbors. Anyone heading toward Birney in the morning is welcome to roll with us. There's no sign-up and no cost.

Younger riders are welcome with a grown-up. We ride at an easy pace and stick together the whole way, so nobody gets left behind.

New to it? Just show up at a stop, or find us at the Trolley Barn Park playground at 8:40 and say hello.

04

Request a Stop

Need a stop closer to you?

The route isn't fixed. It grows with the riders. If a new stop or a small route change would help you and your kids join, we want to hear about it.

To request an additional stop or a route change, join the WhatsApp group and send us a message. Tell us the corner you're thinking of and the days you'd ride, and we'll see what we can work into the morning.
